Mood & Direction

Atmosphere: A Sunday afternoon in a library reading room — warm parchment light, someone's notebook open on the table, a playlist barely audible. Not quiet, exactly. Just unhurried.

Palette logic: Parchment and cream as the base — warm but not yellow. The dark espresso panel is used for hero and footer sections, creating contrast without going full dark-mode. Amber signals everything interactive.

Green treatment: Deep monstera green appears in schedule cards and "find us" sections only — it reads as outdoor/location, grounding the truck's mobility in nature.

Type: Outfit — slightly rounder and warmer than Space Grotesk, still fully geometric. Feels like a well-designed menu or record sleeve rather than a tech product.

Sheet music / tattoo reference: Staff lines embedded as a texture in the dark header. Musical notes as watermark. The dark panel itself echoes a vinyl record sleeve or a leather-bound score.
