Mood & Direction

Atmosphere: A dimly lit room where someone nearby is playing a chord on an acoustic guitar. Warm amber light on dark wood. The smell of espresso cutting through.

Palette logic: Near-black espresso base with ember/copper as the primary signal color. Everything dark, nothing harsh — like candlelight, not fluorescent.

Green treatment: Monstera deep green used sparingly — appears in tags and schedule cards, never dominant. A nod to the plants without competing with the warmth.

Type: Space Grotesk only. Bold weight for display, light for body. No serifs, no decorative faces — the music and tattoo character comes from composition and restraint, not ornament.

Sheet music / tattoo reference: Musical staff lines as section dividers. ♩♪♫ as ambient decoration at low opacity. Typography weight contrast mimics flash-sheet boldness.
